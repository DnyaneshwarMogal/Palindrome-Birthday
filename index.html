Palindrome Birthday

Enter your birthdate and we will tell you if your birthdate is a palindrome.

What is palindrome ? A palindrome is a word/number which reads the same backward as forward. For eg: madam, 12321 reads same when read backward & forward.

Note : This app checks your birthdate in 3 formats yyyy-mm-dd, dd-mm-yyyy, mm-dd-yyyy For eg: if your birthdate is 18 Nov 1997, then app will check for 1997-11-18, 18-11-1997, 11-18-1997
